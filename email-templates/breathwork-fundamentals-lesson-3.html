Before We Begin

Some quick reminders

✦ Find a quiet space where you can lie down, feel safe and won’t be disturbed.
✦ Some people enjoy wearing an eye covering, or having a blanket on them — do what makes you most comfortable.
✦ Check you have a solid internet connection.
✦ Use a good quality set of headphones or speakers to make sure you can hear the webinar.
✦ Don’t eat heavily before the Breathwork.
✦ Have some paper and a pen, or crayons beside you. Many people like to record memories of their experience either with words or pictures after the session.

Important Safety Info · Please Note

Alchemy of Breath is not a medical practice and facilitators are not medically trained. If you suffer from any serious condition you should check with your medical practitioner prior to doing the Breathwork in this course.

The breath style we use is not advised for conditions such as Bipolar, Schizophrenia, acute heart conditions, epilepsy or delicate pregnancy. Likewise, if you are on any medications you are advised to seek medical counsel prior to doing this Breathwork.

If you do have any of the above conditions, please email us at info@alchemyofbreath.com and we can advise you on a different Breathing technique that may be more suitable for you.

Also, it is imperative that you do not drive, operate heavy machinery, work with sharp objects or partake in any other potentially hazardous activities until you feel fully grounded. This may take some time, so please plan accordingly.
