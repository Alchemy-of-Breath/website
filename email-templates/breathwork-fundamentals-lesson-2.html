How to breathe

Dear Family,

It is time for your second lesson in the Breathwork Mini Course, and this is all about how to breathe. We talk a lot about how Breathwork can improve your life, but let’s get specific.

Bringing our full awareness to the breath and inviting more prana or life force energy into the body is the foundation for Breathwork. Breathwork can lead to physical, psychological, emotional and spiritual healing — and some breathers claim just one session was more transformative for them than years of talking therapy.
