Do you still have questions?

Answers to the most frequently asked questions

I have no prior breathwork experience. Can I still join?+

Yes. The training is designed for individuals with varying levels of experience and backgrounds. A comprehensive introductory module guides you through the foundations of breathwork. It is equally valuable for personal growth and for therapists, coaches, and wellness practitioners adding a profound tool to their work.

What is Conscious Connected Breathwork?+

A powerful technique where inhalation and exhalation flow seamlessly without pauses, often accompanied by specially curated music. It calms the nervous system, activates the parasympathetic response, and helps release emotional blockages and limiting beliefs, creating space for deep healing and transformation.

What certification will I receive, and what will I be qualified to offer?+

You receive the Alchemy of Breath Facilitator Certification, a globally recognized qualification. It equips you to facilitate 1:1 sessions, group workshops, and transformative breath journeys in a safe, trauma informed way, in person or online. It is also a strong asset within an existing coaching, therapy, yoga, or bodywork practice.

I'm already a holistic practitioner. How does this help my current offerings?+

Breathwork is one of the most effective ways to help clients release stored emotions and regulate their nervous system, often faster than talk therapy or bodywork alone. Graduates amplify their impact, deepen client transformation, and expand their offerings with breathwork workshops, private sessions, and guided experiences.

Is this only for people who want to become facilitators?+

Not at all. Many participants join purely for personal growth, self-healing, and transformation. The techniques integrate beautifully into coaching, therapy, bodywork, yoga, and holistic wellness professions, or simply into your own life.

What do I need to join this training?+

A willingness to deeply explore and transform yourself. An open mind and the motivation to discover and apply the healing power of breath are essential. No prior experience or professional background is required.
