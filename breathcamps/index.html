Day 7

Integration & closing ritual

Energized and recharged, integrate all the healthy work you've done for lasting well-being — and learn how to keep connected with your process.

Frequently asked questions

Everything practical, answered honestly.

General
Is it possible to do a payment plan?+

Yes! We offer monthly payment installments with a 20% up-front deposit. Please note that your balance must be fully paid 2 weeks prior to your arrival at BreathCamp.

I am very new to breathwork. Is BreathCamp right for someone like me?+

You are so very welcome, at exactly the stage you are at. You'll be surrounded by graduates of breathwork whose speciality is people just like you. The content and care is for two groups — both advanced (our graduates) and the general public at varying stages of experience, many breathing for the first time. You'll be held and taken care of in a beautiful way from the very start.

What is the daily schedule, and when does the last day finish?+

A typical day: morning movement practice, breakfast, breathwork and workshops, group share, lunch, free time, dinner, and evening events. A detailed program guide arrives in your inbox as your camp date approaches. On the final day we finish after lunch, around 2 pm — plan return travel accordingly.

Can I leave sooner than the end?+

If you can't stay for the full 7 days, you are welcome to leave early. Unfortunately, we are unable to offer refunds or discounts for early departure.

Are there conduct rules at BreathCamp?+

Yes. Due to the nature of this work, intimacy is usually at an all-time high — please don't mistake that for an emotional or sexual invitation, and be considerate of others' personal space. Please also keep in mind we are a well-being center: it isn't the place for smoking, drugs, or alcohol beyond the occasional glass of wine. Together, let's protect a safe space for everyone.

Accommodation
What time is check-in and check-out?+

Check-in is from 2:30 pm to 5:00 pm on Sunday — please arrive in time for orientation, which starts at 4:30 pm. Check-out is Saturday from 8:00 am to 9:30 am, and the program ends with lunch around 2 pm (we suggest staying to the very end).

Can I stay off-site, or attend just a day?+

No — all participants stay on-site at ASHA for the full week, so the circle stays together. Day attendance isn't available.

Can I book who I share a room with?+

Potentially! Book together to stay together at the same time, and submit your request on the intake form when you sign up. If you're booking last minute we may not be able to accommodate your request.

Is there wifi at ASHA?+

Yes — free wifi is available in the main house and Dharma Den, though not in the rooms or around the property. We suggest picking up a TIM or Wind SIM card when you arrive in Italy, before coming to ASHA.

Can I bring my pet?+

ASHA is home to 7 cats and 2 dogs. Visiting dogs are welcome with leashes required — strictly enforced — and there's a €40 donation fee per pet. We're surrounded by endless woods where your dog can roam on walks.

Practical
How do I get to ASHA?+

Fly into Rome Fiumicino (FCO). Renting a car is the easiest option — once signed up, you'll get access to your community group for ride-shares. By train, travel toward Chiusi-Chianciano Terme (2–3 changes), then book the local taxi service Il Postalino in advance (ilpostalino.com, WhatsApp +39 335 5424668, roughly €100–120 one way).

What should I bring?+

Loose, comfortable, layered session clothing and house slippers; long pants and sturdy walking shoes; a swimsuit; warm clothes for evenings; biodegradable toiletries; an alarm clock; bug spray; and ear plugs if you're sensitive to noise during breathwork. We recommend bringing your own yoga mat, and travel and medical insurance is suggested.

What's included — and what costs extra?+

Your stay is all-inclusive, except for: airfare, ground transportation to and from ASHA, travel insurance, and à la carte well-being treatments (such as massage or intuitive readings). You may wish to bring extra money for eating out when the group visits the hot springs.

How do I book a private well-being session?+

Life Direction Coaching & private breathwork with Anthony, intuitive readings with Amy, sound healing, massage, and more are available. Contact reception at info@asha.global, or book upon arrival — sessions with Anthony and Amy book up fast.

What is the refund policy?+

We do not offer refunds, and BreathCamp bookings are non-transferable between people (attending a different date may be possible depending on availability). Should we cancel BreathCamp, we refund the full amount minus currency exchange and bank fees. We recommend securing travel insurance. Please read the full terms and conditions before booking.
