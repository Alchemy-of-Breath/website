Questions, gently

New to breathwork? Start here.

What is breathwork?+

Breathwork is the conscious use of your breathing to shift how you feel, think, and heal. At Alchemy of Breath we practice Conscious Connected Breathwork, where inhalation and exhalation flow seamlessly without pauses, calming the nervous system and creating space for deep emotional release.

What can breathwork help with?+

Studies have shown breathwork can help reduce symptoms associated with stress, anxiety, depression, insomnia, and PTSD. Beyond the biological effects, practitioners report greater self-awareness, emotional freedom, and a deeper sense of connection and peace. Breathwork is not a substitute for medical or psychological treatment.

I am completely new. Where do I start?+

Start by breathing with us. We host free live online breathwork sessions every Sunday, open to everyone. They are the gentlest way to experience the work first-hand before choosing a program.

Do I need experience to join a training?+

No. Our trainings are designed for all levels, from complete newcomers to experienced practitioners, and meet you exactly where you are. Many students join purely for their own growth and healing.

What is the Alchemy Regulation Method?+

The Alchemy Regulation Method is our nervous system regulation methodology. You graduate with a daily practice and a framework to regulate your own nervous system, and can qualify to practice as a nervous system regulation coach.

How do I become a certified breathwork facilitator?+

Through the Alchemy of Breath Facilitator Training, a triple accredited certification you can walk two ways: the 8 month online training with weekly live calls, or the 4 month accelerated path that begins with 21 days live at ASHA in Tuscany.
